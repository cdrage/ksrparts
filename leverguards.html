Lever and Hand Guards

These guards are designed to prevent a common and often violent accident from occuring at a track or race day. They are easily mountable with only one bolt and prevent impact against the brake (or clutch) lever.

Lever Guard Features

• Easily mountable, our 1-bolt design allows you to easily slip the lever guard on or off
• All guards are made from a special plastic that is made to be durable as well as flexible enough to withstand a hit
• Mounts to any 7/8" handlebar
• Designed to be used with aftermarket clip-ons (Vortex, Woodcraft, etc.)
