How big are the track maps?

Each track map is 7x7 inches. Depending on the layout, the height may be different. For example, Castrol Raceway is quite "narrow" and is only 2 inches in width, but 7 inches in width.

Each track however is *always* 11mm thick.

What material do you use?

We use PETG infused with Carbon Fiber. Dependin on our supplier at the time, some colourations may vary. Each print however has a "matte black" finish.

Is mounting hardware included?

Yes. Each order comes with 3M VHB strips.

What if the track I want isn't available?

Email us! We'll gladly design and print one for you. We're still actively creating new track layouts to print.
